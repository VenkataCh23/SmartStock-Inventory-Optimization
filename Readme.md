                                                                                   #     **Smart Stock Inventory Optimisation System**
Retail businesses struggle to accurately categorize customers and forecast demand segments based on dynamic sales data, by which they were unable to move a step forward, enabling big players to take advantage of the same. Smart Stock Inventory Optimisation System comes into play, where retailers could also utilise the sales data at their counters to optimise their sales percentage to a maximum extent. They could optimise things ranging from Identifying Most Trending Items for that point of time to Predicting near optimal quantity of stock necessary for them.
